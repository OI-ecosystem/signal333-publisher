Privacy Policy

SIGNAL333 Publisher | Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

SIGNAL333 Publisher is an internal automated publishing tool operated by SIGNAL333 Ventures. This policy explains how the tool handles data in connection with its TikTok integration.

2. Data We Collect

This tool does not collect, store, or process any personal data from TikTok users or third parties. The only data processed is the TikTok access token for the @signal333hq account, stored locally and used solely to authenticate publishing requests.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, device information, behavioural data, or any other personally identifiable information from any user.

4. Third-Party Services

This tool connects to TikTok's Content Posting API. All interactions with TikTok are governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

Access tokens are stored locally on the operator's machine and are never shared with third parties or stored in any external database.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via https://github.com/OI-ecosystem.
